Privacy Policy

User Data Access

This add-on uses user data for the following purposes

  1. This add-on reads files from Google Documents.
  2. It saves the converted Markdown file of the Google Documents' body to Google Drive.
  3. It reads images within the body of the Google Documents and saves them to Google Drive.
  4. Data is not sent or shared to locations other than Google Drive.
  5. This add-on does not utilize any data other than what is mentioned above.

Other Information

To use this add-on, you must agree to the following privacy policy. By using this add-on, you are considered to have agreed to the privacy policy.

  1. This add-on does not collect personal information of the user. However, it may request permissions required to access Google Documents.
  2. The information collected by this add-on will only be used for the improvement, development, and support of this add-on.
  3. The information collected by this add-on will not be provided to third parties. However, information may be disclosed if there are legal requests.
  4. This add-on requires a Google Account. The privacy terms of the Google Account also apply.
  5. This add-on may change its privacy policy at any time at the provider's discretion. If there are any changes, users will be considered to have agreed to the updated privacy policy.
  6. The use of this add-on is permitted only if you agree to the privacy policy. If you do not agree to the privacy policy, please refrain from using this add-on.

本アドオンは以下の用途でユーザーのデータを利用します。

  1. 本アドオンはGoogleドキュメントのファイルを読み取ります。
  2. Googleドキュメントの本文を変換したMarkdownファイルをGoogleドライブに保存します。

  4. データはGoogleドライブ以外の場所へ送信、共有はしません
  5. 本アドオンは上記以外のデータは利用しません。
